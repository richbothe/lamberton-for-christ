Welcome to

We are a Christ centered, Bible believing, independant fellowship located in beautiful downtown, metropolitan Lamberton Minnesota.

Our purpose is to know Him and make Him known. A clear systematic Bible preaching and teaching is our highest priority. We recignize that no other foundation will stand the test of time and eternity.

Our fellowship has a small town friendliness and feel. Come as you are but we hope you do not leave as you were. We strive to leave the imprint of Jesus upon all we meet.

What We Believe

Our Restoration Movement roots look down upon creeds and test of fellowship. We do believe however that a clear understanding of what we stand for, preach and teach should be stated.

  • We believe the Bible to be verbally inspired and completely inerrant upon all subject matters they tough upon. They are the complete, final and only revelation of God's will for the salvation of man and Christian faith and practice.
  • We believe in the triune God. God the Father, Son and Holy Spirit.
  • We believe in Jesus Christ who is fully God and fully man. He was born of the virign Mary, lived a sinless life and died upon the cross as a substitutionary atonement to redeem a lost mankind. He rose from the dead and is now at the right hand of the Majesty on High as our great High Priest and Advocate.
  • We believe in the person of the Holy Spirit, true God, the third member of the Trinity. He is sent to indwell, guide, teach, empower, convincing the world of sin, rightousness and judgement.
  • We believe that man was created in the image of God but fell into sin and now is utterly unable to remedy his lost condition apart from Christ.
  • We believe that mankind can be saved ONLY through the atonement provided by Jesus Christ. This salvation is for all who repent of sin and believe in Him and are born again of the Holy Spirit.
  • We believe in water batism and the Lord's Supper are to be observed by the chrurch. We believe in believer's baptism by immersion.
  • We believe in the second coming of Jesus Christ, a personal, vsible and pre-mellenial return.
  • We believe in the resurrection of the dead; te regenerate to everlasting life and the unregenerate to everlasting punishment.

About Us

Our History

The Lamberton Church of Christ was born out of a series of tent revivals in the late teens and early 1920's. Lamberton Church of Christ was formed around 1923 when our current church building was purchased.

Our Heritage

Lamberton Church of Christ shares a heritage with what is known as "The Restoration Movement". Several phrases describe this mid 1800's church planting movement; such as "No creed but Christ, no book but the Bible" and "In essentials unity, non-essentials liberty and in all things charity". Many different churches carry a similar heritage as the Restoration Movement which spread throughout the midwest and west of the USA.

S.W.A.T. Team

S.W.A.T. Team is our Jr. High/high school youth groups. We work with the Tracy Church of Christ in youth events. S.W.A.T. Team stands for Students With A Testimony. We meet twice monthly rotating between the Lamberton church and the Tracy church. Our activities vary month to month and a detailed scheduled of youth events can be obtained by contacting the church. Our goal is to develop spiritual maturity amoung our youth.
